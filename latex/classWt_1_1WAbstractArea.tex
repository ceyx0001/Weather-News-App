\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Abstract base class for interactive areas in a widget. 

Use an W\+Abstract\+Area (or rather, one of its concrete implementations), to define interactivity that applies on a part of a \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WImage}{W\+Image}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WPaintedWidget}{W\+Painted\+Widget}}. The area may be defined using different shapes through \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WRectArea}{W\+Rect\+Area}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WCircleArea}{W\+Circle\+Area}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WPolygonArea}{W\+Polygon\+Area}}.
