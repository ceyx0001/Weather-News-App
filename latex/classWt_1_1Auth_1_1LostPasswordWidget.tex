\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A widget which initiates a lost-\/password email. 

The widget renders the {\ttfamily \char`\"{}\+Wt.\+Auth.\+template.\+lost-\/password\char`\"{}} template. It prompts for an email address and then invokes \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1Auth_1_1AuthService_ac6d9e43cfbf657c4781f15466d222abc}{Auth\+Service\+::lost\+Password()}} with the given email address.
