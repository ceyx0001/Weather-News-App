The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCornerImage}{Corner\+Image}} is a painted widget with a rounded corner. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCornerImage}{Corner\+Image}} is a dynamically generated image which draws an arc of 90Â°, to represent one of the four corners of a rounded widget.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCornerImage}{Corner\+Image}} is part of the Wt style example.
