\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A interactive area in a widget, specified by a circle. 

The area may be added to a \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WImage}{W\+Image}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WPaintedWidget}{W\+Painted\+Widget}} to provide interactivity on a circular area of the image. The circle is specified in pixel coordinates.
