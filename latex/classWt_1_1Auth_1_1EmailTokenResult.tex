\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The result of processing an email-\/sent token. 

An email token can be used for two purposes\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item the user needs to verify his email address by returning a token sent to his supplied email address.
\item the user indicates that he lost his email and wants to prove his identity by acknowledging an email to a previously verified email account.
\end{DoxyItemize}
