\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The result of resolving a localized string. 

This struct contains the result (in U\+T\+F-\/8 encoding) of resolving a localized string, consisting of its value, its format (Text\+Format\+::\+Plain or Text\+Format\+::\+X\+H\+T\+ML), and a success value indicating whether the string was successfully resolved.
