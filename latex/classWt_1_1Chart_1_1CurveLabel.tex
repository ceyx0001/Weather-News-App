\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A curve label. 

Curve labels can be added with \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1Chart_1_1WCartesianChart_a0ca5407b147a04f3ef3a83fbba8d5b8f}{W\+Cartesian\+Chart\+::add\+Curve\+Label()}}. They are associated with a particular series, and are drawn at the given point in model coordinates. When the chart is transformed (zoom or pan) or the associated series is manipulated, the curve label\textquotesingle{}s position will change, but not its size.
