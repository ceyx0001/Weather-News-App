\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A calendar. 

The calendar provides navigation by month and year, and indicates the current day.

You can listen for single click or double click events on a calendar cell using the \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WCalendar_a51ad8160985f49f5ff0e726df8103921}{clicked()}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WCalendar_a8675afd38f2b773d0945af207d7639f9}{activated()}} methods.

The calendar may be configured to allow selection of single or multiple days using \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WCalendar_a42e800f304acd0e6ed55258149354b9d}{set\+Selection\+Mode()}}, and you may listen for changes in the selection using the \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WCalendar_a317d3018608bbeed934e1823969a3a7f}{selection\+Changed()}} signals. Selection can also be entirely disabled in which case you can implement your own selection handling by listening for cell click events.

Cell rendering may be customized by reimplementing \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WCalendar_aaa45781c155330cc284cd9d54c47bcdf}{render\+Cell()}}.

Internationalization is provided by the internationalization features of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classWt_1_1WDate}{Wt\+::\+W\+Date}} class.
