Process a value. 

Processes a value with model coordinates ({\itshape x}, {\itshape y}). The y value may differ from the model\textquotesingle{}s y value, because of stacked series. The y value here corresponds to the location on the chart, after stacking.

The {\itshape stackY} argument is the y value from the previous series (also after stacking). It will be 0, unless this series is stacked.
